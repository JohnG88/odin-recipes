Arroz con Leche

Description

Arroz con leche is a Mexican delicacy. Great to make duing the fall and winter months. Can use goat milk or cow milk

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Milk
  • Piloncillo(unrefined sugar)
  • Cinnamon(optional)
  • 1-3 leaves of sour orange tree(super rare optional)

Steps

  1. First add rice, cinnamon(if you are using it), and water. Cook until rice is softened.
  2. Add the milk and piloncillo and keep stirring or rice and milf will burn and get stuck to pan
  3. Add sour orange tree leaves(if you are using it)
  4. Bring all to a boil and let cool for a bit
  5. Enjoy
