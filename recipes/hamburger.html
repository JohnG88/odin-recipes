Hamburger

Description

This burger is the best burger this side of the San Joaquin river. Made with the freshest ingredients money can buy.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • American Cheese
  • Black Angus Beef
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Add mayo and ketchup to top bun
  2. Place lettuce to bottom bun
  3. Add beef patty on lettuce
  4. Place cheese on top of beef
  5. Place onion on top of cheese
  6. Place tomato on top of onion
  7. Place top bun on tomato
